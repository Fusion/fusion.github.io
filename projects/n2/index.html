n2

[August 2018 update]

Classical message boards are now a thing of the past. Do not get me wrong! I am still a regular of some sites that use vBulletin (https://forums.watchuseek.com) but these are not technically evolving anymore.

These days, online communities have become mostly support communities and some greate software, such as Discourse or Flarum, have made browsing and contributing simpler.

Look around for a couple minutes and you will also find mixed textual-audio communities and more interesting developments.

n2 is the result of the merger of two exciting forums software: wtcBB and nextBBS.

It’s easy to use and easy to admin.

Lean everything you need to know at http://n2.nextbbs.com

Note: the software is stable and will not lose your data. However some features are still considered alpha quality.
