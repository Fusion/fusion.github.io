Memonic extension patched for Google Chrome

The symptom is pretty simple: you click the ‘Memonic’ extension icon and nothing happens. This patch aims to fix this behavior.

A bit more than three years ago, I published here a patched version of SuperGenPass that would work with Google Chrome. It’s been, since then, downloaded more than 1 million times.

Recently, I also published a patched version of Memonic’s Android app, patched to work with Jelly Bean.

Today, I bring you a patched version of the Memonic clipper that will work even if you have pesky SVG objects in the page you are trying to capture.
