No doubt about that: we are about to hear again about how Android is an inherently insecure OS. It does...

http://www.cs.ucr.edu/~zhiyunq/pub/sec14_android_activity_inference.pdf No doubt about that: we are about to hear again about how Android is an inherently insecure OS. It does not matter that the same algorithm would likely work on other mobile devices or, for that matter, any OS. It’s a clever attack. Well, the “theft” bits of it (invisible activity or photo hijacking) are pretty standard but the first part, the activity detection one, is pretty smart. Note that it’s nothing new though; simply a mix of computer forensics and a timing attack.
